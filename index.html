Projects

A selection of research projects I've contributed to, from single-molecule biophysics to synthetic biology.

Single-molecule protein detection

Ph.D project — Adapted a semiconductor-based sequencing platform into a versatile single-molecule immunodetection system. Femtomolar sensitivity for IL-6 and proteoform discrimination of Alzheimer's-relevant tau variants.

Biophysics Single-molecule Machine Learning Diagnostics

Directed evolution of proteins

SNAP-display for next-generation protein sequencing — directed evolution of binders to improve amino acid coverage, and on-chip single-molecule characterisation.

Protein Engineering SNAP-display

Innovation & design theory

MINES ParisTech — Management science research at Agricool. Intervention research on creative heritage in deep-tech scale-ups. Two publications (AIMS 2022, M@n@gement 2026).

CK Theory Start-ups Suh matrices

Microfluidics for Biology

Droplet microfluidics for high-throughput antibody screening at Sanofi R&D, and 3D irrigated muscle-on-chip development (MyoChip) at Institut Curie.

Microfluidics Organ-on-chip Sanofi

iGEM Pasteur Paris 2018

Gold Medal — Student team design of a biocompatible device against pathogenic biofilms at nerve-prosthesis interfaces. Synthetic biology, microfluidics, and web development.
