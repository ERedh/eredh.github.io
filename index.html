Teaching & Mentoring

Physics Oral Exams — IPESUP

2023 – 2024

Taught weekly 2-hour physics oral examinations (colles) to PC* preparatory class students at IPESUP, in groups of 3–4. A rewarding way to stay sharp on fundamental physics while helping students prepare for the competitive entrance exams.

PSL Week — Synthetic Biology Course

February 2025

Gave a Master-level course during PSL Week on synthetic biology, introducing students to the iGEM competition and presenting my PhD research. A great opportunity to connect scientific research with education.

Alumni Mentoring — ESPCI Paris

Since 2025

Mentoring a 3rd-year engineering student at ESPCI Paris through the alumni network — sharing experiences about research, career paths, and navigating the transition from engineering school to a PhD.

Private Tutoring

2023 – 2025

Two years of multidisciplinary tutoring — physics, chemistry, and biology for first-year medical students, maths at L3 level, and general secondary school support. Teaching is one of the best ways to keep learning.

Outreach & Engagement

Fête de la Science

October 2024

Took part in two outreach activities during the 2024 Fête de la Science. Facilitated a discussion game with secondary school students on the challenges of emotion research, with PSL and the TRACES association. Also presented my career path with SciencesForGirls at Jussieu, encouraging young women to consider scientific careers.

Grandes Écoles au Féminin

Since 2024

Representative for MINES ParisTech at GEF — working on the Charte Mixité, volunteering at events like GEF Startup 2025, and contributing to the "Femmes et Sciences" working group. Also facilitated roundtables for the charter signing at MINES and ESPCI alumni associations.

iGEM Judge

2022, 2023, 2024

Three consecutive years as a judge at the iGEM Giant Jamboree — evaluating synthetic biology projects from international student teams. A brilliant way to stay connected to the iGEM community after competing in 2018.

FIRE Doctoral School Retreat

2023 & 2024

Organised the 2024 FIRE retreat in Annecy from scratch — talks, logistics, transport, accommodation, and activities for 30 people over 4 days. Also organised a thematic seminar on "Sleep and Consciousness".
